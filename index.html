Rapid time-domain infrared parameter space remains unexplored. JWST is NASA's newest frontier mission, and the largest and most sensitive infrared space telescope to-date. JWST's sub-array capabilites allow for time resolutions down to about 0.1 s. Combined with the unprecedented sensitivity of the mission, this opens up a range of scientific possibilities.

Our aim is to enable and exploit timing studies with JWST. The interests of the current consortium members include accretion studies, occultations, pulsating sources, and more. If you are interested in participating in the consortium, get in touch with us.
